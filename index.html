The mean age of the marital categories

Under this, we use the ‘verbs’ “group_by” and “summarize” from the “dplyr” package.

mean_age_by_marital_group <- gss_cat |> group_by(marital) |> 
  summarise(mean_age = round(mean(age, na.rm = TRUE),1))

Visualization of the marital group mean age

Mean_age <- mean_age_by_marital_group |> ggplot() + aes(x=marital, y=mean_age)+
  geom_col(aes(fill = marital)) +
  labs(
    x = "Marital Status of the Women",
    y = "Mean Age (year)",
    title = "Bar Chart Showing the mean age by marital Status of Women"
  )
ggplotly(Mean_age)
